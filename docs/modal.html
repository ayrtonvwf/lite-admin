Modal

Modals are basically pop-ups. They can have lots of scrollable content or even a single sentence. They can hold images, they can be large or tiny, can have header and/or footer or none. They can be plain modals or represent a success message, a danger alert, a warning, anything. Play with some examples bellow:


ScrollableWith header/footer


LargeMediumSmallExtra Small


SucessInfoPrimaryWarningDanger


Take the example code:

<a href="#modal" class="btn-info">Open Modal</a>

<div class="modal modal-info" id="modal">

  <div class="modal-header">My nice modal</div>

  <div class="modal-body">Incredible modal content.</div>

  <div class="modal-footer">That was the modal</div>

</div>
